Can HK become a bike friendly city?

Reported by Jessie Pang, Roy Tang and Shanny Cai


With 218 kilometers of cycle tracks built, cycling has been perceived as a weekend entertainment more than as a mean of transportation. Some point out cycling as transportation is not practical owing to restricted space while some advocating for it. Not long ago, SmartBike programme as a bike sharing scheme was said to be a failure and some track construction projects in the New Territories are delayed. We are planning to discuss how far Hong Kong has gone to promote cycling, whether and how betterment could be found.
